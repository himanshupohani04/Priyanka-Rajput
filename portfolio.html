Project Name: Nudra
We go where learners grow

Project brief: Nudra was designed to bridge the gap between career readiness and accessibility by reimagining how diverse learners engage with learning environments. The system consists of three interconnected components; Sprint (a six-week modular learning program), Tactyl (a physical kit that supports hands-on engagement), and Horizon (a digital platform that extends access and community). Together, they form an inclusive ecosystem that adapts to different learning needs, encourages collaboration, and empowers learners to discover their own pathways to success.

Project Name: Methods of Contextual Research
(Ideal Experience of sleeping)

Project brief: Ideal Experience of Sleeping
This project explored the ideal sleeping experience for students using the Lextant research framework, for which I also earned a Lextant certification.We conducted interviews, observations, and emotional studies, then organized insights through affinitization to identify key patterns and opportunities. To communicate our findings clearly, we created a magazine that visualized the research journey, emotional drivers, and user needs. From these insights, we developed Sleep Tease, a concept brand that transforms tea into a guided sleep-to-wake experience, helping students wake up effortlessly, feel detached from stress, and start their day refreshed.

Project Name: Scadpro X Kohler

Project brief: A collaborative industry project with Kohler focused on exploring the future of hydrotherapy and wellness experiences within the bathroom environment. As project manager, I led a multidisciplinary team in researching user needs, emerging wellness behaviors, and hydrotherapy principles to develop new product concepts that enhance relaxation, recovery, and overall well-being.

Project Name: MACC X SCAD COLLABORATION

Project brief: A collaborative project with the Mahomet Area Chamber of Commerce (MACC) aimed at reimagining their branding, communication, and community engagement strategy. In my role as Campaign Team Lead, I facilitated research, stakeholder workshops, and ideation sessions to uncover insights around visibility, storytelling, and member engagement. The project delivered a refined brand direction, event-based engagement concepts, and digital communication tools designed to help MACC build a stronger, more connected community presence.
